RNAcentral is a new resource to organise data for non-protein coding RNA genes.

RNAcentral offers integrated access to a comprehensive and up-to-date set of RNA sequences. These sequences will be provided by a collaborating group of expert database and supplemented by sequences from the International Nucleotide Sequence Database archives (INSDC). The vision for RNAcentral is presented in the paper by Bateman et al, 2011. The RNAcentral portal provides access to the data via sequence and meta-data search, links together the expert resources, and provides a defined identifier space for individual molecules. In a second phase of development, this reference data will be used to provide annotation for genome sequences from across the taxonomic space.

The development of RNAcentral is being coordinated by the European Bioinformatics Institute, and is funded by the United Kingdom Biotechnology and Biological Sciences Research Council grant BB/J019321/1.

Micro RNAs from miRBase imported into RNAcentral

RNAcentral is pleased to announce the import of 3661 transcriptome assembly contigs representing mature miRNA sequences from miRBase. The accessions are as follows:

  • HAAO01000001-HAAO01001094, Homo sapiens, 1094
  • HAAP01000001-HAAP01001012, Mus musculus, 1012
  • HAAQ01000001-HAAQ01000460, Oryza sativa Japonica group, 460
  • HAAR01000001-HAAR01000359, Drosophila melanogaster, 359
  • HAAS01000001-HAAS01000344, Tribolium castaneum, 344
  • HAAT01000001-HAAT01000254, Bombyx mori, 254
  • HAAU01000001-HAAU01000137, Arabidopsis lyrata, 137
  • HAAV01000001, Ricinus communis, 1

These may be seen by doing a text search for 'miRBase' from the ENA search page.

First RNAcentral data hits the public domain

On 8th July RNAcentral announced the first import of new data from SRPDB. 855 SRPDB entries have been imported as Third Party Annotations using the new "RNAcentral" and "TPA:specialist_db" keywords.

The new accessions are HG322958 to HG323812. These entries may be viewed as individual entries from http://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/HG322958 to http://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/data/view/HG323812.
