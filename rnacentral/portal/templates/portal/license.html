License

RNAcentral wants to ensure that the information is accessible to the public and free to use, for this reason, from version 20 onwards, all data will be released under the Creative Commons Zero license (CC0). This means that any form of reuse of the content is permitted. The data obtained before version 20 remains under EMBL-EBI's Terms of use. For further information, please visit the CC0 page or the FAQ about this type of license.
